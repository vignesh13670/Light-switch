ESP32 Servo Switch

This page uses Web Bluetooth (BLE). On macOS the device list can be weird, so this uses acceptAllDevices and then connects to your service UUID.
If you see many “Unknown device” entries, pick the one that is your ESP32 (try the one that appears/disappears when you power the ESP32).
